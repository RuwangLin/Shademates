## Public materials policy

This repository republishes only a curated subset of the team archive:

- final product screenshots
- team logo
- illustrative persona and empathy map
- early wireframes
- privacy-safe summaries of product, proposal, design, testing, maintenance, and support material
- Android client source with credentials removed

It does not publish student IDs, university emails, contact matrices, private meeting records, individual retrospectives, usability participant recordings, APKs, or the internal governance archive.

## Data and service attribution

The project documentation identifies these external services and sources:

- Mapbox Maps, Search, Directions, and Turf
- OpenWeather
- OpenStreetMap contributors
- City of Melbourne Open Data, including landmark and water-fountain data
- Australian and Victorian public information sources referenced in education content
- Amazon Web Services for the project backend

Each external service remains subject to its own licence and terms. Mapbox/OpenStreetMap attribution must remain visible in map experiences. City of Melbourne data should be attributed according to the applicable dataset licence.
